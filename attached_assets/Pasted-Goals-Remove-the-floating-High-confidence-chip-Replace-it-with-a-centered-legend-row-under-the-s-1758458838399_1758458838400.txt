Goals

Remove the floating “High confidence” chip. Replace it with a centered legend row under the sub-headline (3 chips: Speed, Effort, Confidence).

Add column headers (“Before”, “After”) once at the top (not per row).

Make the grid perfectly symmetric: md:grid-cols-[minmax(0,1fr)_auto_minmax(0,1fr)], equal card heights, even gutters.

Center the Transform spine for every row (no offset), and keep the pill on the spine center.

Tighten visual weight: lighter rose/emerald tints, consistent shadows, identical padding on both sides.

Micro-type rhythm: limit headline width, consistent spacing (mt-8 rows, gap-6), icon + text baseline aligned.

Why this fixes the balance

The legend row recenters the value message and eliminates the odd floating chip.

Column headers once (not per row) reduce clutter and anchor the eye.

The symmetric grid + h-full keeps both sides the same height and weight.

A centered spine + pill gives structure without visual drift.

Matching padding, shadows, and tints make the two columns feel like a single system.
